Frequently asked questions

What kind of apps can I build?

+

You can build native mobile apps for iOS and Android, as well as responsive web applications. Examples include fitness trackers, content management systems, finance apps, task managers, and more.

Do I need coding experience?

+

No coding experience is required! Simply describe what you want to build in plain English, and our AI will generate the complete application for you.

Which AI models does Vibecode use?

+

Vibecode integrates multiple cutting-edge AI models including OpenAI's GPT-5, Anthropic's Claude, Qwen 3 Coder, and Kimi K2. This multi-model approach ensures the best results for your app.

Can I export the source code?

+

Yes! Pro and Enterprise users can export the full source code of their applications built with React Native and Expo.

How do I test my app?

+

You can test your app directly on your phone using the LandingPageM mobile app, or preview web apps in your browser. Changes are reflected in real-time.
